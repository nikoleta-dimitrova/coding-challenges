Who is MGK?
Colson Baker (born April 22, 1990), known professionally as Machine Gun Kelly (MGK), is an American rapper, singer, musician, and actor. He is noted for his compositional blending of contemporary and alternative hip hop with rock and his musical style has been mainly described as hip hop, pop-rap, and rap rock.
Music influences
Baker cites DMX and Eminem as music influences, also listening to rock bands Guns N' Roses and Blink-182 during his youth. In an interview discussing his collaboration with DMX, Baker called the rapper his idol. He also stated that DMX's music helped him through his troubles while growing up.
Latest hits
In April 2019, Billboard reported that Baker's upcoming album would be titled "Hotel Diablo". After releasing it became expremely popular and debuted at number 5 on the Billboard 200 chart, becoming his fourth top ten album.
